About Almas beluga

World's finest caviar Almas beluga, which is derived from a rare Iranian Beluga sturgeon, holds the Guinness World Record for the most expensive food in the world and costs Ksh 3,400,500 per kilogram. It is also the most expensive food in the world.

About Posh Pie

Tender chunks of chicken with pork sausage meat and ham are encased in crisp hot water crust pastry, liberally seasoned with spices and herbs. It is priced at Ksh 50,000.

About Billion Dollar Popcorn

It is made by Chicago-based popcorn company Berco's Popcorn. It is priced at Ksh 500,000.

About White Truffle

White truffles are highly sought after and their market value can vary depending on factors like quality, availability, and demand. It is priced at Ksh 800,000.

About Beluga's Almas Caviar

Almas Beluga caviar is a rare and expensive type of caviar that comes from the eggs of the Albino Beluga sturgeon. It is priced at Ksh 900,000.
